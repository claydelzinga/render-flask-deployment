Why I Built This Program

When I discovered that many AI algorithms struggled with reasoning tasks that required arithmetic and logical sequencing—such as finding combinations of numbers and operations to achieve a target value like 24—I saw an opportunity to explore the problem computationally.

This script takes four numbers and explores all possible arrangements of these numbers combined with arithmetic operations (×, +, −, ÷) to achieve a total of 24.
